What We Do?

There are many NGOs available in our state but many of us don't know that they even exists and if they do then how to contact them. This is where we come in. We bridge the gap between the NGOs and the people by providing a platform which has all the details of all the NGOs availaible, where they are and how to contact them in case if you are planning in donating something!

Who Can Donate?

Anyone, may it be man/woman or any company can donate from anywhere in Goa. All you've to do is contact the nearest NGO and they'll send their volunteer to pick up the items!
